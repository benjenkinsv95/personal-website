About

Hi there. My name is Ben Jenkins, and I'm currently finishing a masters degree in Software Engineering from Harvard Extension School. I expect to complete my degree in Summer, 2018.

While I have experience developing software for robots, I consider myself a software generalist and craftsman. I'm a huge proponent of increasing software quality through developer testing, TDD, BDD, CI/CD, and refactoring.

In 2013, I joined RoboKind as a developer, a company that builds humanoid robots that administer autism therapy. During my four year tenure with RoboKind, I worked on many fascinating features.

The most valuable feature I created was RoboKind's content authoring tools. With roughly 80 hours of my free time, I built the first draft of our content tools. Within the first year of having the tools available, our content authors were able to finish an entire year-long autism curriculum. The completed curriculum led to an increase in children treated and sales.
